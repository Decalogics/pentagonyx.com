"Evangeladum"

A parody of the opening to "Neon Genesis Evangelion", this was made for a streamer named AdumAdumAdum.
(Name so nice you say it thrice!).

This was an ambitious project for me, as it was my first time really editing anything.

Like the original, it features many different plot-significant images flashing on screen to funky music. These are all in-jokes or memes that we made- or people relevant to the streams.

Any peoples depicted, living or dead, are purely coincidental.

Images with Friday Night Funkin' Music


Images with Friday Night Funkin' Music was a Twitter account I made in early 2021. It was what some would call a "gimmick account"- a Twitter account that, as the name implies, has a gimmick that they stick to.

"Friday Night Funkin'" was a free rhythm game that gained popularity in late 2020-2021. It sparked lots of fan-content- and I thought the game was pretty cool, so I made a gimmick account for it.

My first post was immediately retweeted by the main developer of the game (Who I beat in Smash Ultimate twice, by the way), and that got my account on the track to gaining tons of followers.

I kept it up for a while- and even hired someone to post in my place sometimes.

Eventually, it amassed over 8,400 followers. Pretty crazy, right? Well, I decided "Man, I can't do this forever." and shut it down in the summer of that year.

Most of the posts are lost to time- and none of them have survived except for the first ever post.
